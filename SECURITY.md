## Reporting a Vulnerability ("The Leak")

If you discover a hydrostatic leak, memory violation, or security breach in the Variance Core:

1.  **DO NOT OPEN A PUBLIC ISSUE.**
2.  Email `security@blackglass.ai` immediately with the subject line `[LEAK] <Your Topic>`.
3.  Include a proof-of-concept or forensic trace.

### Sovereign Disclosure Protocol
We are committed to resolving vulnerabilities within major variance thresholds (standard: 48 hours). We will acknowledge your report and strictly maintain confidentiality until a patch is deployed.
